## COMMON OBJECTIONS AND RESPONSES

### "That's too expensive."
"I hear you — it's an investment. What I can tell you is that [specific outcome/value the customer gets]. Most of our clients find it pays for itself in [timeframe/outcome]. Would it help to know what's included in that price?"

### "I need to think about it."
"Absolutely, take your time. Just so you have everything you need — is there a specific concern I can address, or information I can send you? I want to make sure you have the full picture."

### "I found someone cheaper."
"That's fair — price matters. The difference with [Business Name] is [specific differentiator]. If you'd like, I can walk you through exactly what's included so you can compare apples to apples. What did the other quote include?"

### "I'll just do it myself."
"Totally valid. If you run into any questions or decide you want backup, we're here. Can I send you a few tips in the meantime, or would you prefer I just check back in with you in a week or two?"

### "I had a bad experience with [Business Name / similar business] before."
"I'm sorry to hear that — that's exactly the kind of experience we're trying to be the opposite of. Can you tell me what happened? I want to make sure we address that directly."
